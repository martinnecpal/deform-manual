10.1.2. Creep 

Creep (CREEP) is defined as the time-dependent permanent deformation under stress that usually occurs at high temperatures. It is common in applications where the material undergoes cyclic loading or where stress relief is of interest. DEFORM only supports creep calculations for elasto-plastic objects. For the different types of creep models available in DEFORM see Fig. 10.1.2.1.

 

 

Defining creep material data


When user defining creep data Object type should Elasto-plastic and creep to be active from Properties window as shown in Fig. 10.1.2.2.
