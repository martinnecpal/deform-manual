DEFINITION

NUCSIZ specifies the size of nuclei during nucleation event.

 

REMARKS

The formation of nuclei would be associated with an energy requirement due to the formation of a new interface and energy liberation due to the formation of a new volume of lower energy material. It can be specified as constant or a function of temperature.

 

Applicable object types:  Elastoplastic

Applicable simulation type: Heat treatment
